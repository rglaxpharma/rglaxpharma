About Us

RGLAX PHARMA, established in 2010, is a leading FDA-approved manufacturer based in India, specializing in ready-mix tablet coating materials. We serve the global pharmaceutical industry with innovative, high-quality excipients that enhance tablet performance, stability, and patient compliance. Our state-of-the-art facilities adhere to GMP, ISO 9001, and USP/EP standards, ensuring sustainable and eco-friendly production.

With a team of experts and a commitment to R&D, we have expanded our portfolio to include film coatings, enteric coatings, and sugar coatings, distributed worldwide through partnerships in North America, Europe, and Asia.

Our Products

Ready-Mix Film Coating

Water-based, instant-mix coatings for rapid dissolution and aesthetic appeal. FDA-compliant and customizable for various formulations.

Enteric Coating Material

pH-sensitive coatings to protect active ingredients from stomach acid, ensuring targeted release in the intestines.

Sugar Coating Solutions

Traditional sugar-based coatings for improved taste and shelf-life, ideal for pediatric and geriatric medications.

All products are backed by rigorous testing and certifications. Contact us for samples and bulk orders.
